Information

Where will the event be held?

The event will be held at the Fontainebleau Miami Beach hotel.

What if I can't make it to Miami?

We're looking into a live streaming option for people that can't attend the event in person. If we can figure this out then those tickets would probably be $25/ticket. If anyone has any suggestions on this please email me at jonah@luptoncapital.com.

What's included in the ticket price?

First off, nobody planning this event including me (Jonah Lupton) will make a single dollar of profit.

100% of the revenues from ticket prices and sponsorships will go directly into making this the best event possible.

Our goal is to have enough revenue from both ticket sales and sponsorships to cover breakfast & lunch on Saturday and Sunday as well as any happy hours and pool parties that we put together. We might not be able to cover 100% of everyone's food & drink costs but we'll do our best.

Special Thanks to WOLF Financial for helping with the planning of this event.
